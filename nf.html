WHO ARE WE?

NeuroNauts is an application designed to empower children with cognitive disabilities by combining cognitive retraining and EEG neurofeedback training together in a single platform.
Users enjoy a user-friendly interface, gamified exercises, and real-time neurofeedback for a holistic experience.
